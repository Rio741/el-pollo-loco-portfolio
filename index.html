How to Play

  • Use 'ARROW KEYS' to move the character.
  • Press 'SPACE' to jump.
  • Collect coins to increase your score.
  • Collect bottles to use as projectiles.
  • Press 'D' to throw a bottle at enemies.
  • Survive the enemies, and defeat the endboss.
